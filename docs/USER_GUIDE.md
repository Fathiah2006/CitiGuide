# CitiGuide — User Guide

A quick walkthrough of the app for new users.

## 1. Getting in
- **Splash** appears on launch and moves you on automatically.
- **Log in** with your email and password, or tap **Create account** to sign up.
  (For the demo, the fields are pre-filled — just tap **Log in**.)
- Forgot your password? Tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email and we'll
  show a confirmation that a reset link was sent.

## 2. Choose a city
After signing in, pick a city — **Benin City, Port Harcourt, Lagos** or
**Abuja**. The selected city is remembered for next time.

## 3. Explore
- The **Explore** tab shows a city hero, **category chips** (Attractions,
  Restaurants, Hotels, Events), a **Featured** carousel and a **Popular** list.
- Tap the city name (top-left) anytime to **change city**.
- Tap the **heart** on any place to save it.

## 4. Search & filter
- Open the **Search** tab (or the search bar on Explore).
- Type a keyword, or browse by category from the suggestions.
- Tap the **filters** button to filter by category and minimum rating, and to
  sort by *Top rated*, *Most reviewed* or *A–Z*.

## 5. Listing details
Tap any place to see:
- A photo gallery, rating and open/closed status.
- **Quick actions**: Directions, Call, Website, Share.
- About, tags, opening hours, phone and website.
- A **map preview** — tap it (or **Get directions**) to open Google/Apple Maps.
- **Reviews** with a rating breakdown.

## 6. Reviews
- Tap **See all reviews** to read every review and mark ones **Helpful**.
- Tap **Write** / **Write a review**, choose 1–5 stars, add a comment and
  **Post review**.

## 7. Favourites
The **Saved** tab lists everything you've hearted. Remove a place by tapping its
heart again.

## 8. Profile
The **Profile** tab shows your stats and lets you:
- **Edit profile** (name, phone, photo).
- Toggle **notifications**.
- **Change city**.
- **Log out**.
